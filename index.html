ABOUT ME

Ivey MSc Business Analytics Candidate 2021

Hi there! My name is Carol. Thanks for visiting my personal website.

I'm a motivated graduate student looking for opportunities to expand my technical repertoire and business acumen. I'm currently enrolled in the Masters of Science in Management, Business Analytics program at Ivey Business School. Prior to enrolling in Ivey, I graduated from the University of Alberta with a Bachelor of Science in Physiology, with First Class Honours. Not too long ago, I was an aspiring medical student and gave up my waitlist position at University of Alberta School of Medicine to pursue my MSc at Ivey!

I have a wide variety of interests - I've performed surgeries in a pediatric neuroscience lab, worked with child victims of sexual assault, and served as Editor-In-Chief of a beloved ugly duckling of student newspapers.

In my free time, I like to stay true to my Calgarian roots and go hiking or climbing. You might otherwise find me huddled in front of the TV, playing video games or watching trashy romcoms.
